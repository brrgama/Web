Blockchain defined:Blockchain is a shared and immutable ladger1 that facilitates the process of recording transactions and tracking assets in a corporative business network. An asset can be tangible (a house, car, cash, land) or intagible (intellectual property, patents, copyrights, branding). Virtually anything of value can be tracked and traded on a blockchain network[1], reducing risk and cutting costs for all involved.
